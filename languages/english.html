Prototype HiperSec

How HiperSec take care of your house security:

TheHiperSec is a prototype electronic lock that opens through mobile devices (your cell phone or tablet), thus keeping the most important thing (your home) in the palm of your hand. HiperSec is not a simple electronic lock, it goes far beyond that, it logs suspicious activities, logs and saves all entry and exit times,so you may have full access to who enters and who leaves your home even from a distance.

How we protect you and facilite your life:

Strange activities will be reported directly to you, such as someone standing too long in front of your door or unauthorized entry. Thus carrying out preventive alerts.

Protect your family by generating an alert every time a person enters or leaves your home after the "danger time", which by default is 10:00 pm.

May access your home from a distance and release access for someone to enter (whether family, friends or employees). This way we reduce the dependence on the use of keys and the forgetting of them.
